Introduction

Atmospheric pollutant levels have a large impact on quality of life. Unfortunately, the countries that experience the worst air pollution levels often have the fewest economic means to improve those levels, and often cannot afford to implement environmental regulations that would limit emissions and improve air quality.

By doing this project, we hope that users will gain a deeper understanding of the factors that contribute to air pollution, as well as the countries that suffer from the worst air pollution and GDP to air pollution ratios. The goal is to discover a correlation between economic output as measured by GDP and air pollution and to analyze the different factors that contribute to air pollution. In response to this information, people can live a greener lifestyle or advocate to living a greener lifestyle
